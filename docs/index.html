Interpretation

The dashboard is a screening tool. A high score means a county has stronger overlap between climate/natural hazards, air pollution exposure, health vulnerability, and weak adaptive capacity. It does not prove that environmental exposure caused health outcomes.

Sources: FEMA National Risk Index, EPA AirData, CDC PLACES, U.S. Census ACS, and Census county boundary files.
